Dress for Success by Augusta Fells Savage Institute of Visual Arts

Support Baltimore City Students Dress for Success!!!

The Augusta Fells Savage Institute of Visual Arts, a SWOWS Community School, is seeking community professionals to donate gently used business attire for our "Dress for Success" Initiative for the 2015/16 school year.

Donate Gently Used Attire

We will provide AFSIVA students with business atire, interviewing workshops and tools to prepare them for business and scholastic endeavors at our school clothing closet and in the classroom.

    9am-2pm
  • University of Maryland School of Social Work
